OMGVA Foundation, guided by His Holiness Akinchan Shree Mitra Shree Ji, is a spiritual movement dedicated to spreading the light of yoga, meditation, and timeless wisdom.

Our Mission & Vision

The foundation is committed to awakening consciousness, promoting spiritual education, and cultivating compassion through yoga, meditation, and service.

Every initiative is designed to empower individuals to discover their inner strength and divine potential.

At OMGVA Foundation, we walk with you on your spiritual journey, offering guidance, wisdom, and community support rooted in yoga, meditation, and selfless service. Whether you seek inner peace, transformative teachings, meaningful connection, or humanitarian involvement, we stand as companions on your path to awakening and compassion.
